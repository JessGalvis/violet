Violet's Power


Violet has the power to read the person’s real intentions and know the future or destiny. When she sees a person, she has a true feeling of what the person is in terms of personality and intentions; also, every time that she goes to sleep she has dreams about the past or future and she can see events as well as people’s choices, thoughts, intentions or actions for the future and in the past.
